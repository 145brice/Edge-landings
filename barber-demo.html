Our Services

Haircuts:

Premium Haircut
This cut includes a straight razor on the back of the neck, a hot towel, scalp massage, and shampoo with a custom style.
Classic Haircut
This cut includes a straight razor on the back of the neck, a hot towel, and a custom style.
Skin Fade
This cut includes a straight razor finish and a hot towel.
Child's Cut
Kids 12 and under. Each cut includes a hot towel and a custom style.
Buzz Cut
1 guard all over. Each cut includes a straight razor on the back of the neck and a hot towel.

Shaves:

Premium Head Shave
This shave is done with a straight razor and double edge razor giving you a super close cut. We start off with pre-shave oil, a hot towel, finish with aftershave, and a cold towel.
Classic Straight Razor Shave
This shave includes pre-shave oil, a hot towel, finish with aftershave, and a cold towel.
Partial Shave
This shave includes pre-shave oil, hot towel, finish with aftershave, and a cold towel.
