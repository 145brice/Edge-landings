Frequently asked questions

Straight answers before you commit. Still unsure? Drop us a note and we'll hop on a call.

Can I cancel after the first month?

Yes. Every plan is month-to-month. If you cancel, we package your site so you can host it wherever you like.

What's the difference between Websites and Leads?

Websites are for businesses that need a professional online presence. Leads are for contractors who want a steady stream of permit-based job leads plus a free website to look professional when reaching out. The Combo bundles Pro Website + Leads at a $109/month discount.

What happens during the 24-hour build?

We write copy, design the layout, wire up forms, and set up analytics. You receive a review link and can request edits before going live.

Do you migrate my current website?

We'll pull over your winning content, update the design, and handle redirects so you don't lose any SEO traction.

How are contractor leads sourced?

Leads come from public building permit data in your local area. We deliver them weekly via email or dashboard access so you can reach out first.

Is the website included with leads really free?

Yes! Your $199/month leads package includes a professional 1-page website with contact form and Google Maps—no extra charge.

Need help choosing a plan?

Tell us about your business and we’ll confirm the right package, timeline, and kickoff date.

After you choose a time, we’ll send a confirmation with next steps.
