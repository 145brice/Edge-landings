Meet Benjamin Carter

Benjamin Carter

Board Certified Personal Injury Trial Lawyer

Benjamin Carter has tried and won cases in state and federal courts across Texas. He is Board Certified in Personal Injury Trial Law by the Texas Board of Legal Specialization, a distinction held by less than 2% of Texas attorneys. Clients appreciate his strategic preparation, calm negotiation style, and relentless pursuit of results.

Outside the courtroom, Ben speaks at Dallas Bar Association seminars and mentors young trial lawyers. He is a graduate of SMU Dedman School of Law and frequently collaborates with medical experts and accident reconstructionists to prove liability and damages.
